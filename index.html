FAQ
Do members need to use real names?
No. You can compete under a performance alias. The Arena is about output.
What proof is required?
Each challenge defines its proof standard. Typically: unedited video + activity verification. (Strava can be optional or required depending on the event.)
When does the leaderboard unlock?
Arena leaderboards are members-only. Open Division shows limited top performances and the winner.
